Hello and Welcome, so today We are going over Origami.

Origami is basically just folding paper into shapes. Thats all you need to know.

Did You Know?

1. Origami is derived from two Japanese words: Ori (folded) and Kami (paper)

2. Akira Yoshizawa is considered the Grand Master of modern Origami. He created over 50,000 origami models, invented wet-folding, and developed
method of diagramming origami instructions.

3. The fastest time to make 100 origami cranes is 40 minutes 35 seconds, achieved by Yoneyama Yuichi (Japan) in Nagoya, Japan, on 30 November 2010.

4. Initially, the art of paper folding was restricted only to ceremonial occasions because paper was scarce and expensive.
